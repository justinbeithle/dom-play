Pride & Prejudice

Lady Catherine Scene

Lady Catherine: If? Do you then pretend to be ignorant of it? Has it not been industriously circulated by yourself?

Elizabeth: I have never heard of it

Lady Catherine: And can you declare there is no foundation for it?

Elizabeth: I do not pretend to possess equal frankness with your ladyship. You may ask the questions, which I may not choose to answer.

Lady Catherine: This is not to be borne. Has my nephew made you an offer of marriage?

Elizabeth: Your Ladyship declared it to be impossible.

Rosings Park Scene

Mr Darcy: I came to Rosings with the single object of seeing you...I had to see you...

Elizabeth: Me?

Mr Darcy: I've fought against my better judgement, my family's expectation...

(PAUSE)

Mr Darcy: The inferiority of your birth.. .my rank and circumstance...

(STUMBLINGLY)

Mr Darcy: all those things.. .but I'm willing to put them aside... and ask you to end my agony...

Elizabeth: I don't understand...

CUT TO REJECTION:

Elizabeth: Do you think that anything might tempt me to accept the man who has ruined, perhaps for ever, the happiness of a most beloved sister?

Longbourn Scene

Mr Darcy: One word from you will silence me forever

Elizabeth is silent

Mr Darcy: If, however, your feelings have changed...

Elizabeth gazes at him

Mr Darcy: I would have to tell you, you have bewitched me body and soul and I love and love you. And never wish to be parted from you from this day on
